Evolution of the collage

The DIC was established in the year 2001 as the Defence Intelligence School (DIS) and operated from a temporary site within the headquarters of DIA at Bonny Camp, Lagos. In October 2005, it was relocated to its current location in Karu, Abuja.
The College was established to carter for the training needs and capacity building of DIA career intelligence officers and operatives to provide real-time defence intelligence in support of the Armed Forces of the Federal Republic of Nigeria both within and outside the country, thereby enhancing national security. This move reflected the vision of the DIA in transforming the school into a centre of excellence for intelligence and security education in Nigeria. Consequently, the school was renamed DIC on 1 March 2013.

About AVM MS Usman CSS

Centre for Strategic Studies

AVM MS Usman Centre for Strategic Studies (CSS) is a multi-disciplinary academic and research centre designed to initiate programmes in strategic studies and undertake security related research. The CSS anchors the Advanced Defence Intelligence Officers' Course (ADIOC) which is designed to broaden officers' knowledge in determination of intelligence in policy and conflict situations.

Also the Center anchors the Intelligence Analysis Officers' Course (IAOC) aimed at raising the quality of analytic tradecraft in the Agency and the intelligence community, It facilitates training programmes with external academic and security organizations through collaboration with relevant institutions. Furthermore, the CSS writes strategic and poilcy papers for the College and the Agency as directed.
